Kai-hotkeys

Execute one of the hotkeys or shortcuts:

  • ctrl+alt+k : console.log('ctrl+alt+k')
  • 1 2 3 : console.log('1 2 3')
  • z+x+c : console.log('name: z+x+c, codes: 90,88,67')
  • c o d e : console.log('name: c o d e, codes: 67,79,68,69')
  • ctrl+shift+v : alert('{17}+{16}+v')
  • t e s t : alert('t {69} {83} t')
